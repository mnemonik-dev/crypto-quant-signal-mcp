◆ How It Works

1

Call Created

Every BUY/SELL call is hashed using keccak256(coin, signal, confidence, timeframe, timestamp, price) at the moment it's generated, BEFORE the outcome is known.

2

Hash Stored

The hash is stored alongside the call in our database. The hash is deterministic and can be independently recomputed by anyone with the call data.

3

Daily Batch

At 00:05 UTC, all new call hashes are assembled into a Merkle tree. Each leaf is a call hash; the tree produces a single root that commits to the entire set.

4

On-Chain Anchor

The Merkle root is published to a smart contract on Base L2 — permanent, immutable, publicly verifiable by anyone.

This means we cannot retroactively edit, delete, or fabricate calls. Every call in our track record existed before the market moved. Verify any call above, or inspect the contract directly on Basescan.
